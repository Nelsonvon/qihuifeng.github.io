About Me

I am a doctoral student in the Knowledge-Based Systems Group (KBSG) at RWTH Aachen University. My research is funded by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG). My supervisor is Prof. Gerhard Lakemeyer, and I am co-supervised by Prof. Jürgen Giesl.

Research

My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, particularly in Knowledge Representation, Multi-Agent Systems, Modal Reasoning, Epistemic Planning, and Neuro-Symbolic AI.
